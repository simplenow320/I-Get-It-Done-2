Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 14, 2025

Our Commitment: I GET IT DONE is built for people with ADHD. We understand the importance of trust and transparency. Your data is your data, and we're committed to protecting it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our task management services:

  • Account Information: Email address and password (encrypted) when you create an account
  • Profile Data: Display name and avatar preferences you choose to set
  • Task Data: Tasks, subtasks, lanes, timing preferences, and completion history you create within the app
  • Usage Data: Streak counts, points earned, achievements unlocked, and focus session statistics
  • Team Data: If using Team mode: team member information, delegated tasks, and collaboration notes
  • Voice Data: When using voice capture, audio is processed for transcription and immediately deleted after processing
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for troubleshooting and compatibility

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Provide the task management and productivity features of the app
  • Sync your data across devices when you sign in
  • Send notifications you've opted into (reminders, focus mode alerts)
  • Calculate and display your progress statistics (streaks, levels, achievements)
  • Enable team collaboration features if you use Team mode
  • Improve app performance and fix technical issues
  • Respond to your support requests

3. Data Storage and Security

We take security seriously:

  • All passwords are encrypted using industry-standard bcrypt hashing
  • Data is transmitted using HTTPS encryption
  • Our database is hosted on secure, enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure
  • We conduct regular security reviews and updates
  • Access to user data is restricted to essential personnel only

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to power app features:

  • Deepgram: For voice-to-text transcription (audio is processed and not stored)
  • OpenAI: For intelligent task extraction from voice input (text only, no personal data shared)
  • SendGrid: For sending password reset and support emails

These services have their own privacy policies and handle data according to their terms.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Data may only be shared:

  • With team members you explicitly invite in Team mode
  • When required by law or valid legal process
  • To protect the safety of users or the public
  • With service providers who help operate the app (under strict confidentiality agreements)

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data at any time
  • Correction: Update your profile and task information within the app
  • Deletion: Request complete deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Notifications: Control all notification preferences in your profile settings
  • Export: Request an export of your task data

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@simplenow.co.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion:

  • Your personal data will be deleted within 30 days
  • Anonymized, aggregated data may be retained for analytics
  • Backup copies are purged according to our backup rotation schedule (maximum 90 days)

8. Children's Privacy

I GET IT DONE is designed for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. International Users

Our servers are located in the United States. If you access the app from outside the US, your data may be transferred to and processed in the US. By using the app, you consent to this transfer.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when the policy was last revised.

Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: info@simplenow.co
Support: Visit our Support page
