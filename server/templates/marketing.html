The 4-Lane System

A simple structure that reduces overwhelm and keeps priorities clear

NOW

Today's focus. Short list, clear priorities. No decision fatigue.

SOON

Next few days. Visible but not urgent. Smooth transitions.

LATER

Future weeks. Captured, not forgotten. Out of today's mental space.

PARK

On hold. Safe zone for ideas. Reduces guilt, preserves creativity.
