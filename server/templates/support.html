Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password" and enter your email. You'll receive a link to create a new password.
Is my data backed up?
Yes! All your tasks and progress are automatically synced to our secure cloud servers in real-time.
How does the 4-lane system work?
Tasks are organized into Now (today), Soon (next few days), Later (future weeks), and Park (on hold). Tasks automatically move between lanes based on their timing.
Can I use the app on multiple devices?
Yes! Sign in with your account on any device to access all your tasks and progress.
How do I cancel my account?
Contact us at info@simplenow.co and we'll help you with account deletion. Your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.
